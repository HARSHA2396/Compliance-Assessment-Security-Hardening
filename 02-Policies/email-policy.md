# Email Security Policy

**Policy ID**: SEC-EMAIL-001  
**Effective Date**: January 2026  
**Review Date**: January 2027  

---

## 1. Purpose

To establish standards for secure email usage, prevent data loss, protect against phishing, and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties using organizational email systems.

## 3. Email Account Management

- Strong password: Minimum 12 characters, uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA): Required for all email access
- Password expiration: 90 days
- Account lockout: After 5 failed login attempts for 30 minutes
- No credential sharing
- Automatic logout: 15 minutes of inactivity

## 4. Acceptable Use

- Business purposes only
- Internal collaboration and documentation
- Professional external communications
- No personal use or forwarding to personal accounts

## 5. Prohibited Content

- Personally identifiable information (PII) without encryption
- Intellectual property without authorization
- Financial information (credit cards, bank accounts)
- Confidential or restricted data
- Discriminatory or offensive content

## 6. Security Requirements

- Email encryption: Required for confidential communications (S/MIME, TLS)
- Transport security: TLS 1.2 or higher for all transmissions
- Email authentication: SPF, DKIM, DMARC enforced
- Attachment scanning: All files scanned for malware
- Phishing detection: Email gateway scanning enabled
- Suspicious email reporting: Immediate escalation to Security team

## 7. Data Handling & Classification

| Classification | Encryption | Restriction |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Optional | No restriction |
| Internal | Recommended | Internal only |
| Confidential | Required | Limited distribution |
| Restricted | Required | Approval required |

## 8. Email Retention & Archival

- User mailboxes: 3-year active retention, 7-year archive
- Automated archival: Daily backups
- Litigation hold: 5-year retention when requested
- Archive access: User + authorized administrators only

## 9. Mobile Email Access

- Device registration in MDM required
- Device encryption mandatory
- Auto-lock: 5 minutes inactivity
- Biometric or PIN authentication required
- Remote wipe capability enabled
- Corporate data only (no personal use)

## 10. Third-Party Access

- Guest accounts: Temporary, expiration date set
- NDA requirement: All vendor email access
- Contract termination: Access revoked within 24 hours
- Audit trail: All vendor email activity logged

## 11. Monitoring & Compliance

- Email gateway scanning: Malware, phishing, data loss
- User activity logging: 90-day minimum retention
- Compliance audits: Quarterly review
- Incident response: 2-hour investigation SLA
- Training: Annual security awareness mandatory

## 12. Incident Reporting

- Suspected phishing: Report to security@company.com immediately
- Data breach: Escalate within 24 hours
- Suspicious activity: Contact IT immediately

## 13. Enforcement

- **Violation Types**: Credential sharing, unencrypted PII, non-compliance
- **Consequences**: Warning, access suspension, or termination per severity
- **Appeal Process**: Contact Security Manager within 5 business days

## 14. User Responsibilities

- ✓ Maintain password confidentiality
- ✓ Enable and maintain MFA
- ✓ Verify recipients before sending sensitive data
- ✓ Encrypt confidential communications
- ✓ Report suspicious emails
- ✓ Complete annual security training
- ✓ Comply with data classification standards
